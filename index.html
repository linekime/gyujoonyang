Gyujoon Yang


Gyujoon Yang is an artist who moved to New Zealand in 1997 and later returned to Korea in 2012. He graduated from Chung-Ang University's Graduate School of Western Painting and continued his studies at the Auckland Graduate School and the Whitecliff School of Art in New Zealand. Since 2005, he has been presenting his work as an author of Whitespace, one of New Zealand's leading galleries.


During his artistic journey, Gyujoon Yang was immersed in the "Light" series until 2004. In 2005, he released the "Medium" series of calligraphed calligrapher, showcasing a vivid expression of tension with high concentration.
